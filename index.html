How it works

One platform. Three parties. Zero admin.

Sabba connects employers, employees and side quest vendors in a single integrated marketplace — from policy to booking to payroll.

🏢
Employers

Subscribe & manage

Set annual side quest allowances, review and approve bookings in your HR portal, and manage vendor verification — all in one place.

  • Live dashboard with real-time booking metrics
  • One-click approval or rejection of bookings
  • CSV bulk employee import (500 per upload)
  • Analytics, messaging and custom email templates
🧭
Employees

Discover & book

Browse a curated marketplace, pick a payroll spread (3, 6 or 12 months), and book side quests within their employer-funded allowance.

  • Side Quest quiz for personalised recommendations
  • Interactive 3/6/12-month cost breakdowns
  • Sabba Points rewards and redemption
  • Community feed with travel matching
🗺️
Vendors

List & fulfil

Reach thousands of employer-funded buyers. Manage bookings, send trip details and track earnings through a dedicated vendor portal.

  • Package management with drag-and-drop images
  • Booking pipeline with HR approval integration
  • Earnings dashboard with commission tracking
  • 8–12% commission — paid by Sabba, not consumers
The platform

Built end-to-end. Live today.

Sabba is a fully deployed, production-grade platform — not a prototype. Every feature below is live at sabba.app.

🏘️

Community social feed

Employees post about upcoming side quests, like and comment on each other's content. A travel matching algorithm connects employees booking the same package within ±30 days.

🏆

Gamification — Explorer to Legend

Five-level progression system with daily points caps. Employees earn Sabba Points for bookings, posts, comments and likes — redeemable against future bookings (100pts = £1).

📋

My Bookings — tabbed layout

Upcoming, Pending, Past and All tabs with counts. Full booking history, payroll progress bars, HR approval status and one-tap access to booking details.

💳

Payroll-integrated checkout

Employees choose their payroll spread before adding to cart. Sabba Points redemption panel sits above the fold. Stripe card checkout also supported.

📧

Transactional emails via SendGrid

Booking submitted notifications to employee and HR admin. Booking approval emails to employee. In-app notification system throughout.

🔒

Security & compliance

Rate limiting, HSTS, X-Frame-Options, Referrer-Policy. GDPR-compliant privacy policy live at /privacy. Multi-tenant architecture with full data isolation per employer.

⭐

Platinum sponsored listings

Four premium sidebar ad slots in the Community page — two per panel. Separate from marketplace featured slots. A distinct vendor revenue stream managed by Super Admin.

⚡

Performance & UX

RouteProgress bar on every navigation. Skeleton loading screens on Home, Marketplace, Bookings and Allowance. Fully mobile-responsive across all three portals.
