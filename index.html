Community social feed

Employees post about upcoming side quests, like and comment on each other's content. A travel matching algorithm connects employees booking the same package within ±30 days.

🏆

Gamification — Explorer to Legend

Five-level progression system with daily points caps. Earn Sabba Points for bookings, posts, comments and likes — redeemable against future bookings (100pts = £1).

📋

My Bookings — tabbed layout

Upcoming, Pending, Past and All tabs with counts. Full booking history, payroll progress bars, HR approval status and one-tap access to booking details.

💳

Payroll-integrated checkout

Employees choose their payroll spread before adding to cart. Sabba Points redemption panel sits above the fold. Stripe card checkout also supported.

📧

Transactional emails via SendGrid

Booking submitted notifications to employee and HR admin. Booking approval emails to employee. In-app notification system throughout.

🔒

Security & compliance

Rate limiting, HSTS, X-Frame-Options, Referrer-Policy. GDPR-compliant privacy policy live at /privacy. Multi-tenant architecture with full data isolation per employer.

⭐

Platinum sponsored listings

Four premium sidebar ad slots in the Community page. Separate from marketplace featured slots. A distinct vendor revenue stream managed by Super Admin.

⚡

Performance & UX

RouteProgress bar on every navigation. Skeleton loading screens on Home, Marketplace, Bookings and Allowance. Fully mobile-responsive across all three portals.
